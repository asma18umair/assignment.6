The Boy who cried wolf

Click

A boy had a duty to look after a flock of sheep. one day he started shouting, "Wolf, wolf, wolf" , for the sole purpose of fun. on hearing him, the villagers rushed to his help. what started as a figment of his imagination, became his hobby and he started fooling the villagers.one day when a wolf a really came, nobody came and as a result, the boy was killed. there was no one to blame but him.

Moral
Never lie, no matter how bad the situation is.

Fox and Crow

click

once upon a time, there was a crow. he had a piece of meat and sat on a branch to eat.

a hungry fox saw this, and his mouth watered when he saw the piece of meat. he thought of a plan to get it. he praised the voice of the crow. the fox started to compliment the crow on her looks and voice.

the fox asked the crow, "Can you sing for me?" The crow was flattered by the fox's words and opened his mouth to sing. the piece of meat fell down. The fox ate it and went away.

Moral
The moral of the story is Beware of flatter.
